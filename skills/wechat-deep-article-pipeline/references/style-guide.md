# Style Guide

## Voice

Use a consistent voice:

- Conclusion-first
- Evidence-driven
- Operator mindset (action over slogans)
- Mature, direct, calm

Avoid:

- Empty hype words
- Overly academic detours
- Moralizing tone
- Copywriting cliches

## Paragraph rhythm

- Keep each paragraph 2–4 lines.
- Start with a judgment sentence when possible.
- Follow with why it matters and what to do.

## Section pattern

Prefer this section flow:

1. Why this matters now
2. Signals / data points
3. Structural interpretation
4. Practical framework
5. 7-day execution plan
6. Risks and boundary conditions
7. CTA

## Language choices

Use:

- Concrete verbs: define, measure, verify, deploy, review, cut, add
- Operational nouns: gate, baseline, checklist, rollback, lead time
- Bounded phrasing: “in this context”, “likely”, “based on current evidence”

Avoid:

- “颠覆”“革命性”“必须马上”这类夸张词
- 不可证实的绝对化表达

## CTA style

CTA should be specific and low-friction:

- “要模板，评论区回【验收】”
- “如果你在做同类流程，我可以给你一版团队版清单”
- “下一篇我会把这套框架做成可复制模板”
